# 💫 About Me:

I believe the best technology is built to solve meaningful problems and create lasting impact. My work is driven by curiosity, innovation, and a commitment to turning complex ideas into practical, user-focused solutions.

My interests span Artificial Intelligence, Finance, Data, and Product Engineering, where I enjoy combining technical expertise with strategic thinking to build intelligent systems that improve decision-making.

I approach every project with a research and product mindset—understanding the problem, designing scalable solutions, validating assumptions, and continuously refining the outcome. I value thoughtful execution over unnecessary complexity, and meaningful impact over simply shipping features.

I value innovation over imitation. Instead of recreating existing projects, I prefer building products that introduce new ideas, address overlooked problems, or improve existing solutions in meaningful ways. I enjoy experimenting with modern technologies, validating ideas through prototypes, and iterating until they deliver real impact.
